A volunteer records a case into the Miracle Messages database

Volunteers (you!) pick up cases from the trello board. Thoroughly review the information given on the case.

Make sure the client is still contactable by contacting them directly or the volunteer who reported the case. If contacting the client directly, you can ask about text, email, or any alternative way to stay in touch once you finish the case.

You search the internet based on information from the Trello board.

Some resources caseworkers are using include:
  • White Pages for a preliminary search
  • Pipl for cross referencing, finding emails, verifying given phone numbers, or finding any organizations the loved one may be in.
  • Infospace pulls up internet appearances
  • Facebook for information and possible contacts
  • Ancestry for connections
  • Hometry gives you the option of contacting a loved ones neighbor if you can't reach them
Tips:
  • Take little pieces of information from different sites
  • Play around with the filters on search sites. Given information may be slightly off due to memory
  • It is better to having several possible contacts rather than one contact that you're sure about
  • Get creative! Past caseworkers have used prison system databases to find contacts

Make calls to multiple possible loved ones when found. Leave voicemails, texts, or even a letter so they can follow up with you!

Script for calling the loved one:

Hi, my name is [your name], and I’m calling on behalf [client]. I work with a program called Miracle Messages, that helps people reconnect with loved ones. [client] asked us to help him reconnect with [loved one], so I’m trying to reach [loved one]. Is this the right number?

If you can reach them through text or voicemail, make sure to include:

Please let me know either way so I can ask our volunteers to continue the search if need be. You can reach me at _[phone number, email address, etc]_. Thank you!

Script for emailing or sending a letter to the loved one:

Hello, My name is [your name] and I work for a non-profit organization called Miracle Messages that helps individuals reconnect with their loved ones. I have a message from [client] who is looking for his brother [loved one]. I am sending this letter in hopes that this is still his current address/email. If you are the intended recipient please give me a call at [phone number, email, etc.]. If this address is incorrect please feel free to give me a call to let me know as well. If you'd like to reach our organization by phone, please call the Miracle Messages hotline at 1-800-MISS YOU. Best wishes, [your name]

Return address:

Miracle Messages, C/O Bespoke Coworking
845 Market St. #450A
San Francisco, CA 94103

Update your case every Monday with your results

It's useful to have a saved folder with case information in the event people reach out for information in the future.

Connected with loved one and they want to reconnect:
Pass on any pre-recorded message and reconnect them! 		Connected with loved one but no desire to reconnect:
Let the client know that their loved one does not wish to contact them at this time. 		Case closed:
Let the client know you were unable to reach their loved one and will be closing their case for now. 		Loved one is deceased:
Let the client know. It can be helpful to include the loved one's obituary and a way to seek a counseling service like 211.org.

Report any difficulties and results during the Wednesday 5:30pm case working conference calls!
